Starbucks vs Coffee Day: customer loyalty analysis.

A polished case-study section for Rania’s profile. The analysis is framed from customer behavior, café choice drivers, loyalty triggers, and marketing opportunities. Numerical values below are marked as a portfolio model so she can replace them with survey data when final research responses are ready.

Starbucks

Premium café positioning built on ambience, personalization, global brand recall, app-led loyalty, seasonal beverages, and lifestyle-led social sharing.

★

Café Coffee Day

Accessible Indian café network with familiar menu, value comfort, casual meetings, and high nostalgia among students and working professionals.

☕

Choice drivers: Starbucks vs Coffee Day

Relative importance across customer decision factors. Replace portfolio-model values with survey results later.

Insight 1 — Starbucks wins lifestyle recallCustomers often associate Starbucks with premium ambience, customization, Instagrammable experience and work/study comfort.
Insight 2 — CCD can win value-led repeat visitsCoffee Day remains competitive when customers want affordability, familiarity, quick meetings and low-pressure café time.
Insight 3 — Loyalty is not only priceRepeat visits are influenced by app rewards, consistency, staff experience, seating comfort, product innovation and perceived status.
Rania’s analyst angleConvert café visits into measurable KPIs: NPS, visit frequency, average ticket size, redemption rate, repeat purchase, and churn risk.
